 AI Personalities: Aggressive vs. conservative drivers with varying risk profiles (e.g., overtaking, pit strategy).

 Driver Fatigue & Morale System: Fatigue affects performance; morale changes with results.

 Team Orders: Implement team strategy decisions like letting one driver pass another.

 Mechanical Failures: Simulate engine, gearbox, or suspension failures beyond just tyre issues.

 Overtaking Probability Based on Sector: Different overtaking chances per sector and DRS zones.

🌦️ Weather & Environment
 Live Weather Map: Sector-based rain simulation (one sector wet, others dry).

 Track Temperature: Affects tyre degradation and strategy.

 Weather Radar Forecast: With limited accuracy, requires guesswork.

🔧 Simulation Mechanics
 Fuel Management: Light vs. heavy fuel loads affect speed and pit strategy.

 Tyre Temperature Simulation: Influence grip and wear depending on pace and compound.

 Driver Skill Evolution Over Time: Drivers improve or decline during seasons.

📊 Analytics & Visualization
 Lap Time Delta Chart: Compare lap times of selected drivers.

 Telemetry View: Sector speed, tyre data, and pace graphs per lap.

 Interactive Strategy Dashboard: Click to change tyre or pit strategy in real-time.

🛠️ Technical / Code Structure
 Modular Architecture: Split into race_engine, ui, drivers, teams, weather, etc.

 Config File: Load circuits, teams, and driver stats from .json or .yaml files.

 Replay System: Save and replay races.

🌍 Multiplayer & Expansion
 Hotseat Multiplayer: Two human players alternating pit and tyre decisions.

 Online Multiplayer: Socket-based simple multiplayer for race control.

 Career Mode: Start as a rookie team and build up over seasons.

 Fantasy League Mode: Manage a team like F1 Fantasy – select drivers, score points weekly.

💾 Persistence & Storage
 Save/Load Feature: Resume unfinished seasons or races.

 Season History: Track results, stats, and performance by year.
